    Program Overview:

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Technology program offers students a comprehensive understanding of computer science principles and their applications in various fields. Through a combination of theoretical coursework, practical projects, and hands-on experiences, students develop the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving field of technology.

    Structure:

    Core Courses:

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Computer Architecture
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Algorithms and Complexity
  • Computer Networks

    Elective Courses:

  • Web Development
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Cybersecurity
  • Cloud Computing
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Game Development
  • Robotics
  • Human-Computer Interaction

    Program Duration:

4 years (8 semesters)

    Admission Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirement

    Career Opportunities:

Graduates of the B.Sc. CSE program are prepared for a wide range of career opportunities in both the public and private sectors.

  • Software Engineer/Developer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Network Administrator
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Web Developer
